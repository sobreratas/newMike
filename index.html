About

Miguel Sanz Jiménez obtained a PhD in English Linguistics at Complutense University Madrid (First Class). His dissertation studies the Spanish translations of African American neo-slave narratives. His main area of research is literary translation, particularly dealing with the portrayal of alterity and African American Literature. He holds a Master’s in this field, as well as in Teaching English as a Foreign Language.

Dr. Sanz Jiménez has been involved in a variety of different projects including novels, audiobooks, comics, blogs, subtitles, and interview translations.
